Vincaelos

Appearance

The Vincaelos people are characterized by their tall and lanky frames, velvet-like fur, horns and hooved feet. These horns can vary in shape or number but typically occurs in either a pair or threes. They can have up to 2-4 eyes, sometimes in an asymmetrical layout. Underneath their masks, their faces are soft and cow-like. Bony, armor-like protrusions occur on their body, typically on their torsos, forming rib-like patterns. Their tails typically are medium-length but can rarely be longer. They have tufts of fur on the tip, thought to help with subtle body language. Fur is often one-color but markings can occur, like stripes, splotches or spots.

Typical Vincaelos are colored in dark cool colors, ranging from grey colors to nearly black. Eyes are bright and vivid colors.

Diurnal Vincaelos are often colored warm, mottled tones, such as reds and browns. Eyes are similarly warm colored, ranging from bright reds, yellows, oranges and rarely blues or purples.

Biology

They reproduce sexually and rarely bear children, due to their long lifespans. Their species has little sexual dimorphism with most Vincaelos having the biology to both carry or sire a calf.

The Vincaelos are omnivores, eating whatever is available in their desert planet, but eat mainly plants. They practice a form of agriculture in which they use artificial light and underground springs to grow crops. They eat whatever meat they can capture but rarely keep animals.

They have short fur because their warm home but are mainly nocturnal, being able to generate their own body heat to fend off the night chill. Diurnal mutations do better in the heat and as a result are diurnal. Many like to live on the surface, unlike others who work underground.

Art

Vincaelos have a rich artistic culture. The most obvious point is in their masks, which they receive once they are mature and know at least how they self-identify. They decorate and change their masks, using it to express themselves and use as an identifier. They most often use it as a self-identifier but it can be used to show their accomplishments, tell a story or even as a status symbol. They rarely replace their masks but its acceptable if they feel like they have made a big enough change. Those that replace them often or wear other masks than their own are seen as deceitful. It’s seen as a point of shame to wear an undecorated mask as an adult.

Lifestyle

Most Vincaelos are driven by a thirst for knowledge. Underneath the planet are many libraries, dedicated to knowledge of both their people and others, tended to by diligent librarians. Many others travel in the pursuit of knowledge, bringing it back to store in their great libraries. There is a stereotype that they are actually a conquering people which is only based of their wayfaring ways.

Technology

Despite their peaceful status, they have a rich technological prowess. Their best technology exists in the ability to open up “pocket realms” where they often use memories and knowledge to build settings based on either different planets or even the past. Some are skilled enough to cobble together wholly original realms.

Most libraries have physical volumes which are digitally backed up on a “cloud” that is based in a server in a place unknown, the knowledge fiercely guarded from outsiders.

They also have skilled agricultural skills, able to grow a vast ability of plants underground in any season.

Language

The Vincaelos language doesn’t have a name and is only spoken between other Vincaelos, as they have the ability to make the melodic songs that make up the language. Each’s name is heard to the human ear as some sort of music.
