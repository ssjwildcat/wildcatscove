Cyber Demons

“Cyber Demon” is a catchall term for all peoples not native to Cyberspace who changed due to their exposure there. The most common way this happens is people from other worlds or dimensions coming to Cyberspace and staying there for extended periods of time. Passage into the dimension isn’t a common practice with only a few intentional gateways being maintained. Unintentional portals can appear, often in areas where the Veil (or what is known as the barrier between worlds) is thin. With enough power or skill, one could “punch” through the Veil. However, attempting such a thing without proper knowledge is extremely risky.

Cyber demons are called such due to the mutations that occur to someone with prolonged exposure to the space. These mutations can range from discolored skin, tails, horns, and many other effects. They tend to look demonic, which is where the misnomer originates. Mutations are known to get more drastic or exaggerated as time goes on but rarely cause any discomfort. There’s rumor that said mutations can be induced on purpose in some way but is discouraged due the lack of knowledge of what the process entails.

Cyber demons can pass back to their home dimension (such as an Earth native going to Cyberspace then back again) but mutations will not disappear once developed. In fact, these mutations can even pass down to children who may never enter Cyberspace but can fade farther down the family line. On the flipside, cyber demons who remain in Cyberspace may see more traits manifest farther down the line until they are indistinguishable from native born cyberfolk.

As far as its known, cyber demons all originate from alive and well people but stories suggest a secondary way of becoming one. The stories say that Cyberspace and other dimensions may sometimes accidentally act as a net from the waking world to whatever comes next. Souls that get caught in this net pass through and, without a body, reality is forced to construct them one, building upon their memories and self-perception. This new body however is always colored by the dimension they find themselves in, meaning they will most certainly no longer appear exactly the same as they did in life.

However, such a process hasn’t been proven but appears in fiction as a popular variant in the ‘isekai’ genre. If any cyber demons who originated from a deceased soul exist, then their status has escaped notice of the greater populace.
