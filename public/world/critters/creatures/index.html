Creatures

*“Creatures” is often used in place of “people”.

Cosmotalians come in all shapes and sizes, both mundane and fantastical! This is your guide to some of the more fantastical ones.

Mammals

Felines / Cats


Ventarian Cats ,also known as flying cats, are simply cats with wings. Despite their wings seeming too small to fly, magic allows them to.

Spinaen Cats, also known as spined or horned cats, are cats with some manner of spikes, horns or other bony growths. Ones with hard growths or spikes at the end of the tail are sometimes called “Dingmauls”!

Flora Cats are cats that feature flowers as part of their body which they use for photosynthesis to supplement their diet. This also includes Cactus Cats which have a reputation for being drunkards.

Felisacans are stocky cats with lynx, lykoi and sabertooth features. They are known for their ability to turn into a more bestial second form.

Canines


Ventarian Dogs, also known as flying dogs, are dogs with wings. Despite their wings seeming too small to fly, magic allows them to.

Spinaen Dogs, lso known as spined or horned dogs, these are dogs with some manner of spikes, horns or other bony growths. Horned dogs are sometimes called “Hellionhounds”!

Lycans are stocky wolves which are known for their ability to turn into a more bestial second form.

Lagomorphs


Jackalopes are rabbits with either horns or antlers.

Skvaders are rabbits with fully functional wings.

Wolpertingers are rabbits with horns and antlers, they are thought to have originated from a jackalope and skvader union.

Carbuncles are rabbit-like creatures with forehead gems and the ability to dreamwalk. It’s thought they might be related to the gemonyms of Brink.

Gemonyms are rabbit-like creatures from Brink with gems set in their body and various inborn magical abilities who live underground.

Bats


Draculbats are oversized bats with fangs that have the need to feed on unnatural food sources like magic, energy, dreams or, most stereotypically, blood.

Mustelids


Kamaitachi are weasels with retractable wrist spurs and sometimes bony spikes on other parts of their bodies

Other


Island and Saturn Devils are sister species of caprine creatures with horns, wings and tails. Island Devils hail from the island of Satchu and trend towards darker colors and leathery wings while their sister species, the Saturn Devils, are the original species from Saturnia and trend towards lighter colors and feathered wings.

Reptiles


Dragons are a species of extremely varied subspecies that also seems to have a deep connection to magic, thanks to their breath attacks and ability to subsist on magic alone.

Birds


Rocs are giant eagle-like birds native to Cazcairn and Pural but have since migrated all over Cosmotalia.

Amphibians


Salamandrals are salamanders with an affinity towards fire, thus making them naturally immune to fire, lava and heat.

Chimera

Chimera are any creature with features taken predominantly from two animals. These can naturally occur as hybrids between drastically different parents. Some chimeras are included with other groups on this page (like wolpertingers) while these are too split down the middle to fit to either group. These are chimera that are common and can further procreate.


Griffins are chimera with feline and avian traits that may or may not have wings.

Enfields are chimera with fox and avian traits that may or may not have wings.

Vaewolves are the exceptionally rare hybrid of a lycan and a draculbat.

Sea-dwelling


Dobarchu are otters with fish features that make a fulltime life underwater possible.

Other


Shapeshifters are ink-based creatures with, as the name implies, the ability to shapeshift.
