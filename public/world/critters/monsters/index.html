Monsters

Not all is fun and games in the wilderness of the world. Monsters are not uncommon and can be quite dangerous—though not always. They are characterized by their often bestial appearance and violent behavior. They can be hunted for resources, food or sometimes just glory. Some believe some monsters were former creatures changed by magical corruption.

Mammalian Monsters

Serpopards are massive leopard-like creatures with a long snake-like neck, cobra-like hood and venomous fangs. They are found in the desert regions of the world.

Wargs are bestial wolf-like monsters that inspire fear. Some believe they’re the result of lycans losing control when transformed.

Ursus are large bear-like monsters with a long, slamming tail and found rarely in the wilderness of the world. Superstition says to say their true name is to summon them.

Urstrixes are large chimeric creatures with avian and bear-like features, found in the wilderness of the world though most commonly on Alis and Borau. They're thought to be related to the ursus.

Barghest are large, dark dog-like monsters that banshee often hunt with. Gentler individuals can be found hanging around graveyards and churches.

Calopus are large chimeric monsters with deer, cat and wolf-like features and characterized by backwards-facing horns they use to attack with. They're rather dangerous but are often hunted for meat.

Perytons are winged deer-like monsters with fangs who, despite appearances, are quite vicious and mean. Efforts have been made to tame them as a mount but so far it seems to be a fool’s endeavor. They're not even good for eating as their carnivorous diet makes their meat bitter.

Questing Beasts are rare chimeric monsters with lion, leopard, snake and deer traits. It's extremely elusive and skittish so it’s often hunted for glory.

Avian Monsters


Cockatrices are chimeric monsters with chicken and draconic features. Its stare is paralyzing as long as eye contact is held.

False Cockatrices are similar to the cockatrice, being smaller chimeric monsters with chicken and draconic features. It lacks its cousin's paralyzing gaze and are kept for eggs and meat.

Banshees are dark-feathered bird-like monsters with a shrill screaming cry which superstition says heralds death. It’s bird-like appearance makes it uncanny.

Pheonixes are rare bird-like monsters known for their functional immortality in which they die in a burst of flames that they later are reborn from.

Quanixes are rare bird-like monsters known for their functional immortality in which they die in a burst of ice that they later are reborn from.

Stymphel Birds are metal-feathered territorial birds that are often farmed for their feathers. They live in large flocks which can easily overwhelm lone creatures. Legend says Purrlloy often uses them to send messages.

Draconic Monsters


Snallygasters are small and annoying chimera with bird and dragon features and a tentacled face which conceals teeth inside.

Tarrasque

 are massive and rare dragon-like monsters with a turtle’s shell who are so rare and destructive that every appearance is a historical event. Legend says they can be soothed by singing but it’s unclear how effective this truly is.

Abberant Monsters


Ooze are magically animated slime mold colonies found in dark and damp areas with high magical concentrations. They have some shapeshifting capability which is used to capture prey which they often mimic to draw them in closer—though its rarely successful due to how flawed their mimicry is. They prey on anything they can catch, including sapient creatures. They can be caught and used for their culinary and medicinal properties.
