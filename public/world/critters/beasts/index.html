Beasts

Thereâ€™s a wide variety of non-sapient creatures in Saturnia which are often domesticated, hunted or otherwise kept around for one reason or another. They are often passive but not always.

Unikirns are horse-like creatures with draconic features which are used for transport all over Cosmotalia.

Gigant Moths are giant moths, which are often kept for their silk or for certain sacred practices.

Gigant Bees are giant honeybees, which are often kept for their honey.

Gigant Eels are giant eels, which are sometimes used for aquatic transport or their meat (which is an acquired taste).

Pronghorns are antelope-like beasts kept for transport.

Pelagorns are large sea-faring bird beasts with serrated beaks. They are kept by fishermen, who use them to retreive their catch.

Hippogriffs are chimeric beasts with feline, equine and avian traits. They are rarely tamed as transports as they are largely territorial.

Hippocampi are chimeric beasts with fish and horse traits. They are gentle-natured and often used as underwater transportation.

Pseudodrakes are tiny dragon-like beasts kept as pets or for sending messages. They come in a wide array of species, morphs and colors.
