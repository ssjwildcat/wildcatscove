Envy


Deadly Demon of Envy

Personality

Ruled by his vice, Envy is well, envious. As a result, he’s nearly never happy and obsessively coveting. He takes a sadistic joy out of taking things from others, often taking the most sadistic route to do so. Underneath that though, he’s a sniveling coward who doesn’t like direct confrontation, rather preferring underhanded methods.

Abilities

His primary ability is that he can induce envy in others, so much so to the point they will look past their own morals and willingly hurt others they care about to get the object of their desire. His current alignment is Ice, giving him the ability to freeze parts of his body and freezing venom.
