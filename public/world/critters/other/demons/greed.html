Greed


Deadly Demon of Greed

Personality

Greed, like her siblings, is driven by her sin and in her case it’s greed. She wants and wants, giving little care to what others think or how they’re affected. Even her loyalty is only given because what she can get out of it.

Abilities

Her primary ability is that she can induce feelings of greed and want in others. Her current alignment is Mind, giving her the ability to hypnotize her foes for a short amount of time
