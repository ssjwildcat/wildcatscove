Demons

Demons are mysterious entities created by large scale tragic, sorrowful or traumatic events. There’s a misconception they are created of evil, most certainly colored by the circumstances of their birth.

Their appearances vary wildly but often appear with traits of multiple creatures, monsters and beasts with implausible traits. Their appearances are influenced by the event that created them, like a flood giving its demon aquatic traits. The one trait all demons share is their sclera are black.

All demons have magical abilities influenced by the event of their origin. To use our flood demon, this demon might have control over water or storms.

All demons are also beholden by their nature to perpetuate the pain and sadness of the event that created them. As a result, they are often aggressive and violent beyond reason. Some guilds, such as [[GIEM]]’s demon-hunting subguild, are created solely to put down demons before they can cause much damage. However, it’s difficult but not impossible for a demon to break free of its nature. While it’s not entirely known how they do, as demons who do that remain elusive, some speculate that by mimicking the creatures around them and suppressing their violent nature, they may conquer their nature.

The Seven Deadly Demons

The most famous demons are the Seven Deadly Demons, said to have been the first demons created, as a direct result of Inketch’s exile. Some of Inketch’s final words alluded to this as they had apparently warned the Sentinels their actions would “introduce calamity into the world”. It’s largely believed that this means their exile and the intense emotions involved created the Seven Deadly Demons and perpetuated the existence of demons at large. Due to their creation involving all thirteen of the Sentinels and Inketch, they are the most powerful of their kind.

Each of the seven is said to embody a particular vice and has the unique ability to influence others to indulge said vice. This is their primary ability and the only one they can call their own.

Possibly due to their creation involving the Sentinels, the Deadly Demons can absorb magic from their victims and gain an ability depending on the Element of what they’ve stolen. However, they can only hold one ability at a time and must discard it to gain a new one.

Immediately after their creation, it’s said the Seven Deadly Demons rampaged across Saturnia, both directly and indirectly harming the newly independent Saturnians. The Lost Order of the Thirteen Knights waged a years-long campaign to defeat until they were able to corner all seven and imprison them using their own power; a callback to how the Sentinels banished and sealed away Inketch. The site of their sealing is a mystery to this day.

However, it seems the seal has either failed or been breached because there’s been sightings of them across Saturnia...
