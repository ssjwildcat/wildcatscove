Lust


Deadly Demon of Lust

Personality

As the demon of lust, Lust delights in getting others to do what she wants by subtle coercion and impression, subconsciously using their desires against them. Notably, she doesn’t force others under her control, though whether this is a limitation of her powers or something she chooses to do is unknown. Also notably, she is one of the seven demons who seems to be less single-minded, having her own wants and desires which might be due to her nature.

Abilities

Her primary ability is that she can use illusions to appear differently and can make herself more impressible to others. These powers seem to pick up on her victim’s subconscious. Like her siblings, she can induce her vice, lust, in others though seems to prefer manipulation over this. Her current alignment is Sound, giving her the ability to unleash a shrill sonic scream to incapacitate foes.
