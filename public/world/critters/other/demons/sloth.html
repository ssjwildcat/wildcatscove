Sloth


Deadly Demon of Sloth

Personality

As the demon of sloth, Sloth is lazy and lethargic. He’s somewhat of a coward, aware that he’s not able to go toe-to-toe like his siblings can, something he is ashamed of. He tends to misdirect and dodge both combat and general tasks as a result. He doesn’t find much joy out of what he is, only finding it a chore to be leashed to another.

Abilities

His primary ability is that he can induce lethargy into others, sapping away their energy and motivation. He can also retract his claws. His current alignment is Mist, giving him the ability to turn invisible.
