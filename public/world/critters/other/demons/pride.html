Pride


Deadly Demon of Pride

Personality

Pride, as her role demands, is deeply prideful and full of herself. She constantly flaunts herself and brags about her very many great attributes. When felt she’s been demeaned, insulted or otherwise sullied, she flies into a wild and dangerous rage. She lacks any sort of true caring for anyone else, even her own siblings who she sees as allies more than anything.

Abilities

Her primary ability is that she can entrance others to herself or induce pride in others, often to the point where they neglect those they care about. Her current alignment is Metal, giving her the ability to eject her feathers as sharp, metal projectiles.
