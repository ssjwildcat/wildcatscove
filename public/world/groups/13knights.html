The Thirteen Knights

The 13 Knights are a Saturnia-based group of legendary figures that only arise in dire circumstances, when the Sentinels deem their intervention is needed. These figures are blessed by their patron Sentinel ultimate mastery of their Element and an infinitely large magical well to utilize it with. Whether or not this magical well is actually infinite or just gigantic isn’t confirmed.

There has been only three recorded orders of Knights.

The Forgotten Order

The Forgotten Order was the first order of the 13 Knights, picked personally by the Sentinels before they went into hibernation at the end of the mythic age. Their role was specifically to combat Inketch’s prophecized evil. They also assisted the Elemental Monarchs with a lot of freedom given to them. Uniquely among not only the Knights but any creature, they innately understood their power and how to use it. Because of this, they were known to take on many students.

Their greatest adversary was the Seven Deadly Demons, a set of demons who emerged after Inketch’s exile, as well as many other minor demons who appeared in the years after. It’s known that after a years-long hit-and-run campaign, they sealed the Deadly Demons away in a location lost to time.

Their final act as a group was to found the Guardians of Saturnia, a group of intelligent and skilled creatures who would watch over Saturnia in their absence and find the next order when they arose.

The Bulwark Order

The Bulwark Order was the second order of the 13 Knights which arose because the threat of invading dragons from Dracana. Due to this, they became heavily involved in the Saturnia-Dragon War by leading battles and defending settlements. After the War ended, their efforts shifted to integrating peaceful dragons into the community and driving off any lingering antagonistic ones.
