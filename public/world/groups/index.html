Groups

Thirteen Knights: Orders of thirteen creatures blessed by the Sentinels who only arise in dire circumstances to defend Saturnia

Saturnian Guardians: A group of creatures, traditionally nine but can vary, who monitor Saturnia and assist in solving problems and crises

The Elemental Monarchs & Champions: A group of four creatures appointed by the Sentinels to lead the newly formed Saturnia after their hibernation and their descendants, the Champions who battled the Ebony Wolf

Guilds

GIEM: Standing for Guild Investigating Eccentric Matters, this guild investigates problems related to various supernatural or otherwise unexplainable phenomena

Seven Seas Prestige: A guild made up of shipmasters who coordinate crews, ships and other pertinent information. Made up of various chapters based on coast.

Religious Groups

The Wolves of Mount Howlmore: A group of mostly wolves who live on the sacred mountain, Mount Howlmore and revere the Sentinel of Lightning, Howlmore. Also take in Lightning-aligned creatures to tutor them in using their abilities.
