Howlmore


The Sentinel of Lightning, The Vindicator

  • Element: Lightning
  • Color: Bright Blue
  • Patron to: Fighters, Warriors, Veterans, The Accused
  • Associated Season: Mid-Summer
  • Personality: Strict, Just, Stubborn
  • Common Pronouns: he/him, they/them
  • Sacred Land: Mount Howlmore
Other Information

It’s said his storm cloud can shoot off lightning, sometimes affected by his mood.

His most popular depiction blends elements from wolves, hawks and clouds. He is often depicted with a halo of lightning.

His title comes from his just nature and how he leads others into battles.

Mount Howlmore is named for him. Howlmore itself is a portmanteau of “howl” and “Rushmore”, as in Mount Rushmore.
