Purrlloy


The Sentinel of Metal, The Visionary

  • Element: Metal
  • Color: Copper
  • Patron to: Writers, Architects, Painters, Artists in General
  • Associated Season: Late Autumn
  • Personality: Artistic, Generous, Overdramatic
  • Common Pronouns: they/them, she/her, he/him
  • Sacred Land: The Purris Highlands / Purris
Other Information

It’s said they can eject the quills on their wings for defense.

Their most popular depiction blends elements of wyverns, raptors and sharks. They are often depicted with a halo of metal spikes.

Their title comes from their artistic nature.

“Purrlloy” comes from a portmanteau of “purr” and “alloy”.
