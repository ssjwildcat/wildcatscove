Greenstone


The Sentinel of Air, The Maven

  • Element: Air
  • Color: Yellow
  • Patron to: Librarians, Broadcasters, Teachers, Scribes
  • Associated Season: Mid-Winter
  • Personality: Diligent, Disciplined, Knowledgeable
  • Common Pronouns: they/them
  • Sacred Land: The Greenstone Grassland
Other Information

It’s said they can predict changes in the weather using their two long tail feathers.

Their most popular depiction blends elements from songbirds and dragons. They are also commonly depicted with a halo of swirling wind.

Tales speak of their desire to freely share knowledge.

The Greenstone Grassland is named for them. Greenstone is simply a portmanteau of “green” and “stone”. Meta-wise, the place came before the god, which makes their name a misnomer.
