Chillark


The Sentinel of Ice, The Peacekeeper

  • Element: Lightning
  • Color: Cyan
  • Patron to: Counselors, Hikers, Rescuers, Hunters
  • Associated Season: Early Winter
  • Personality: Quiet, Slow to Anger, Peaceful
  • Common Pronouns: they/them, he/him
  • Sacred Land: Chillark Woods
Other Information

Chillark is uniquely depicted as bipedal and quadrupedal. Often, his quadrupedal stance is associated with the rare occasion he’s angry or his more “wild” aspects.

Their most popular depiction blends elements of bears and goats. He is often depicted with a halo of ice.

Their title comes from their desire to keep the peace and ability to resolve problems cleanly.

Chillark Woods is named for them. “Chillark” itself is a portmanteau of “chill” and “ark”, which is a corruption of “arctic”.
