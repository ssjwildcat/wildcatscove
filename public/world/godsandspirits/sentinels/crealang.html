Crealang


The Sentinel of Sound, The Harbinger

  • Element: Sound
  • Color: Orange
  • Patron to: Couriers, Forecasters, Musicians, Singers
  • Associated Season: Late Summer
  • Personality: Alert, Watchful, Creative
  • Common Pronouns: they/them, she/her
  • Sacred Land: Fountain Glen
Other Information

Crealang is consistently depicted as the largest Sentinel.

Her most popular depiction blends elements from rocs, hyenas and lizards. She is often depicted with a halo of visible sound waves.
