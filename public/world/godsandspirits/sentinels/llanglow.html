Llanglow


The Sentinel of Mist, The Smuggler

  • Element: Mist
  • Color: Grey
  • Patron to: Thieves, Tricksters, Liars, Exiles
  • Associated Season: Late Winter
  • Personality: Mysterious, Covert, Unknowable
  • Common Pronouns: they/them
  • Sacred Land: The Llanglows
Other Information

Llanglow is consistently depicted as the smallest Sentinel.

Their most popular depiction blends elements from tuataras, foxes and birds. They are depicted with a halo of mist that can obscure their face.

Their title comes from how it’s said they can hide anything or anyone and move covertly.

The Llanglows are named for them. “Llanglows” itself comes from “Llanos” and “glow”.
