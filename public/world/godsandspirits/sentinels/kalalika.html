Kalalika


The Sentinel of Fire, The Rallier

  • Element: Fire
  • Color: Red
  • Patron to: Blacksmiths, Soldiers, Advisors, Mentors
  • Associated Season: Early Summer
  • Personality: Headstrong, Determined, Temperamental
  • Common Pronouns: they/them, she/her
  • Sacred Land: The Kalalika Desert
Other Information

Many depictions depict her pheonix feather mane on fire.

Her most popular depiction blends elements from rams, salamandrals, lions and pheonixes. She is often depicted with a halo of fire.

Stories speak of her great voice which can whip others into a frenzy, rally others, inspire her allies and confuse her enemies.

Kalalika is considered the primary god of the pantheon in the [[Conflagno Kingdom]], where they further associate her with the sun and life.

The Kalalika Desert was named after her. Kalalika itself is a corruption on “Kalahari”.
