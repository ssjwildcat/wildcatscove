Just about nothing can get Vice down and with good reason. They’re in a good place in their life, with more money than they’d like to admit, a job they love and living on the beach they love. While they get a bunch of colorful characters coming through the Hibiscus Lounge, they are happy to meet them. After all, it’s how they met their partner.

Personality

One would be very hard pressed to get Vice down or even bother them at all. Very few things actually bother them and even so, those things usually just elicit dislike, not hate or anything like it. They are just simply too chill and this makes them a very likable person—which is perfect for a bartender. They occasionally come off as distant, which isn’t the case. They simply are a pretty private person and rarely talk about themselves. They are very prim and polite and don’t often break this, even if they’re annoyed. In fact, it’s very difficult to tell when they’re bothered.

History

Vice was born into money with a pair of supportive parents, who were happy to support all their endeavors. Once they were an adult, they moved a little while away to fulfill their dream: to open a bar. Their parents were happy to provide the starting funds for what would become The Hibiscus Lounge. Everything after that is self-earned. They don’t tend to brag about any of their wealth and or even make any indication they have it—well, other than their fancy beachside mansion.

Vice grew up with their sister Nova, happily doting on her and being a big influence on her. She often visits them and helps around the Hibiscus Lounge.

While bartending, they met Conrad who was a regular. After the other demon going overboard one day and not leaving at closing, Vice took pity on him and took him home to recover. Before passing out, he admitted he liked Vice—then passed out. In the morning, Vice mentioned it, assuming it was a drunken ramble. Conrad said it wasn’t so soon after, they started spending more time together, eventually becoming an item.

Skills

As a bartender, they have a good sense of what tastes good and mixes well. They also know a lot about different alcohols. They have a photographic memory, making remembering drink combos a breeze. They’re very flexible, able to get their body into many weird positions. They do a lot of yoga to keep themselves in shape.
