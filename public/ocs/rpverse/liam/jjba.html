Personality

Liam is a standoffish and distrustful man to the extreme. He fears others plotting against him (something being a Stand user has only worsened). He struggles to control his emotions that tend to run extreme and overwhelming. As a result, he can be defensive and hard to approach. It’s not only others he fears but himself, as he harbors angry and violent thoughts. His greatest fear is flying off the handle and hurting someone, knowing how others view him would be forever changed. When around others he likes, he is still quiet and pensive, often enjoying the company even if he’s off in his own world.

History

Pre-teens rather than adolescents, Liam and LP’s trip to the frozen lake went differently. When LP dared his twin to step out onto it, Liam took the dare, determined to make his twin stop bothering him. However, he fell through instead. According to LP, when he pulled him from the water, his arm had been impaled on a curious golden Arrow and a wound that manifested itself onto him too. Liam didn’t remember any of this, having gone into a feverish comatose state for about a week after the incident.

When he finally did awake, he found that both him and his twin had been gifted with special abilities, something they’d later find was called a “Stand”. More than a decade later, Liam struggles with having a normal life. Things only go downhill when he is confronted by a man calling himself “Sketches”, who claimed to have created the Arrows and blamed the twins for losing the one they had found. Strongarmed into helping him along with his twin, Liam fears the stress will make him do things he would regret.

Skills

Outside of his Stand powers, Liam likes to program though rarely finds the time to dabble in it nowadays. Him and his twin have a sort of “twin telepathy” and can sense when the other is near or feeling strong emotions.

Stand - Paranoid Android

Paranoid Android (though sometimes referred to as simply ‘Paranoid’) is a massive cyborg bird Stand. It’s an aggressive and twitchy Stand with a wealth of weapons, such as its spear-like beak, teeth, claws and razor sharp wings. While it is a formidable Stand that can manifest within 6 feet (or 2 meters) of Liam to fight, it’s true ability is also dangerous. Within a 30 feet (or just over 9 meters) radius around Liam, Paranoid Android can cause all others to violently hallucinate terrifying visions. While it is a formidable Stand, Liam struggles to control it under extreme duress and thus, is reluctant to use it.

Paranoid Android interacts in a curious way with Electric Friend, the latter able to plug its plug-like tail into a port on Paranoid Android’s chest. When this happens, Paranoid’s speed and strength are augmented and Electric Friend can help guide Paranoid. Visually, the pair switch color highlights, with Paranoid’s red highlights turning green and Electric Friend’s green highlights turning red. Before initiating, the twins must be in complete agreement and “in-tune” with the other. This isn’t an ability the twins can use often however, as they become exhausted after the pair disconnect, leaving them vulnerable.

Relationships

LP

Liam does love his brother, he really does, but sometimes he can be...overbearing. He knows he's unstable and can't be alone but he craves independence sometimes. The protectiveness goes both ways; LP can't defend himself with Electric Friend but Liam's Paranoid Android is more than enough to protect them both. At the end of the day, no matter what, LP might be the only person Liam truly trusts.
