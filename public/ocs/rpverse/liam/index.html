Liam is a person prone to bad luck and doesn’t have a lot of faith in the world. His stable family is about the only thing keeping him from going adrift in his own mind. But age had made him wiser, has made him actually want to live. If only it was simpler.

Personality

On the outset, Liam appears distant and standoffish, often like he’s “off in his own world”. He appreciates being left to his own devices most of the time. He’s inherently distrustful and only trusts a handful of people. He tends to be snarky and rude, though it can depend whether its genuine or just playful. He’s hyperemotional, feeling his emotions very intensely. He’s easy to anger and even easier to make cry. He often attempts to close himself off and rarely shares information about himself, fearing what others may do with it. Those few he does trust find him a quiet but well-meaning person who clumsily but sincerely cares for them.

History

Liam was born to parents Konnie and Daisy, along with his twin Luigi. Him and his twin were very close, with Liam being the follower to his twin’s leader. Liam’s life was fairly typical up until his teenage years. It was then his twin had an accident. The pair were wondering around in the woods near their home in the late wintertime. LP dared his brother to step out onto a frozen lake, playfully antagonizing him to not be a coward. Liam took this close to heart, trying to get him to let up until he lashed out, shoving his brother away—and out onto the ice. The deceptively thin ice and impact of his fall meant LP went straight through the ice into the cold water. While paramedics were able to fish him out, he ended up succumbing later that night.

This event sent Liam into a tailspin. Despite his family insisting it was just an accident, Liam pinned blame squarely onto himself. Symptoms that were relatively minor worsened quickly and it wasn’t long before he went into a full-blown psychotic breakdown. It wasn’t until he tried to end his own life, reasoning “he should have died instead”, that anyone found out his behavior was more than just grief.

When his twin reappeared a year later, Liam struggled to believe it was actually him and not some creature sent to torment him. In fact, it wasn’t until he landed on a treatment that brought him back to stability did he begin to let LP back into his life.

Liam ended up graduating and, after a couple years spent at home, decided to strike out on his own. He wanted to be independent and, even with how he struggled holding down a job, he kept with it, even if his parents would always welcome him home.

Sometime when he was 22, he met Sebastian, someone with similar problems, albeit with a less supportive family. He grew very attached to him and eventually the two began to date. This would lead to Liam seriously seeking help for his temper.

Alternatively, Liam would meet Mr. Sketches and despite being intimidated of him, found himself enamored because the other treated him kindly (perhaps moreso than one would treat their fuckbuddy?). Somewhere along the series of one-night stands between the pair, they got closer than they meant to.

Skills

Liam is a self-taught programmer who fiddles with it for fun but would like to pursue it professionally. He’s good with computers, both on the hardware and software sides.

Due to an interest with reptiles since he was young, he’s very good at keeping them, as he has a leopard gecko.

Trivia

If it isn't painfully obvious already, he's the favorite OC.

Liam is officially diagnosed with schizophrenia and due to it, has insomnia.

Liam has a leopard gecko named Cleopatra, though he typically calls her Cleo.

Liam collects sweaters and likes them a lot. It’s partially because they make him comfortable and warm but also because they hide the self-harm scars on his arms.

He is severely allergic to citrus.

One way he attempts to cope is smoking. He says he can quit any time but frankly, has not tried.

He drinks a lot of coffee (probably more than what is healthy). He drinks it black or completely sweet. No in-between.

When he doesn’t have sleeping medication, he tends to take a lot of walks and drives at nights.

Despite being an easy crier, he despises other people seeing him cry. He fears to be called a crybaby and also he just isn’t a pretty crier.

Due to his mother Konnie being half-Italian, he himself is 1/4th Italian. He knows Italian though is pretty out-of-practice.

Liam loves horror and monster movies.

Liam’s first concept circa 2014 was as a human version of LP, initially named Liam Pierce and created for AUs. After growing attached, I divorced him from LP and made him his own character. Then the two became related again after I made them twins.
