Personality

Without the exposure and isolation of Cyberspace, he’s a more stable individual, being temperamental and pessimistic still but coming off as grumpy rather than flat out angry. He generally has little patience and can come off as easily annoyed. His brother Liam is one of the few exceptions to this as he views himself as his brother’s keeper and responsible to keep him safe. If you know him well, you’ll find someone clumsy but well-meaning.

History

Since he did not die in an accident (his mother Konnie having found them before he ventured out onto a lake), the Piccoli family is a more whole family. LP would prove to be a stabilizing presence in Liam’s life, having recognized his brother was having trouble before he spirals into a full-fledged breakdown in his teens. The two would graduate and end up sticking together into adulthood before LP decides he doesn’t need to be on constant vigilance with his brother.

Skills

LP would use his knack for computers and learn to code traditionally, becoming a freelance coder.
