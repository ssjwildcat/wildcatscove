LP’s life is so damn weird. He was alive until 15 then died for a bit then came back as some sort of cyberspace demon? He can’t tell you how it happened and frankly, is just happy to be alive. Alive or not, he still struggles with what exactly it means to no longer be entirely human.

Personality

LP, even at his nicest, comes off as grumpy and grumbly. Its very easy to make him get defensive, especially when he’s nervous or scared and he’ll even try to run away from situations that scare him. Casually, he as a light-hearted, if cynical and sarcastic, vibe to him. He is truthfully very lonely and tries to make nice with people, in hopes of making friends. He also tends to be lazy and likes using the easiest solution to solve his problems. While he doesn’t outwardly show it, he is pretty self-conscious of how he looks and doesn’t like to be compared to a demon.

History

Despite how he looks today, LP was once wholly human. He was born to parents Konnie and Daisy, along with twin Liam. He was always the leader to Liam’s follower and often would try to get him to do risky things. His life was fairly typical until their teenager years, when he had an accident. he pair were wondering around in the woods near their home in the late wintertime. LP dared his brother to step out onto a frozen lake, playfully antagonizing him to not be a coward. Liam took this close to heart, trying to get him to let up until he lashed out, shoving his brother away—and out onto the ice. The deceptively thin ice and impact of his fall meant LP went straight through the ice into the cold water. While paramedics were able to fish him out, he ended up succumbing later that night.

LP, miraculously and mysteriously, awoke within the realm of Cyberspace, changed but alive. Young, alone and scared, he was forced to fend for himself. However, within the year, was able to figure out how to teleport back out and go home. Furious at Liam all this time, his anger dissipated when he found Liam had become paranoid and unstable. He even refused for a time to believe it was him, something that hurt. But he did eventually let him back in and the pair would reconcile.

Now an adult, he enjoyed his independence, moving out of his parents’ home first. But on his own, old traumas resurfaced with little ability to reconcile, making him a somber adult. However, few would know this, due to his snarky exterior.

After being dragged along to a carnival he didn’t plan going to by Alabaster, he found himself attracted to him, despite himself. He always found excuses to be with him and even realized that Ally was having a profound and positive effect on him. He wishes to become at least somewhat better because of him.

Skills

His transformation into his current form has left LP with a few powers. He can teleport, levitate a short distance off the ground and manipulate technology and electricity. He has an accelerated healing factor, able to heal faster than the average human. He can summon small objects to himself. He has a very small pocket dimension he can use to store and grab a handful of items.

He’s good with computers, even without his cyber abilities. He also has an interest in gardening, thanks to his momma Daisy. He has a surprisingly green thumb.
