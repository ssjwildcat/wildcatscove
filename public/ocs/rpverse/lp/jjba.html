Personality

A good deal cheerier than his counterpart, LP retains his standoffish and cynical nature. He does crave companionship and has been known to be a bit of a “suck-up” to get people to like him. Once you see the true him, you’ll find him good-natured, even if he likes his sarcastic jabs. The best way to get him on your side is to appeal to his curious nature—though you might have to endure his excited ramblings on whatever has grabbed his interest this week. He seems to lack a good sense of self-preservation in the name of knowledge, diving down deeper and deeper rabbit holes to satisfy his curiosity.

History

Pre-teens rather than adolescents, Liam and LP’s trip to the frozen lake went differently. When LP dared his twin to step out onto it, Liam took the dare, determined to make his twin stop bothering him. However, he fell through instead. LP immediately went to fish him out, suffering a mysterious wound on his arm as he did. When he got Liam out, the impossible cause was there embedded in Liam’s arm; a large, golden Arrow. Wanting nothing to do with it, he removed it and tossed it into the brush where he thought he’d never have to worry about it again.

Liam fell into a feverish sleep for nearly a week but when he woke up, he was perfectly and impossibly fine. And they had been granted a unique and potent power, one LP would later find out was called a “Stand”. Now, more than a decade after, he has a life he enjoys, watching over his brother and using his abilities to make things easy. That is, until Liam told him he had gotten them into a situation: someone who claimed to have created the Arrow was making them help him retrieve it and all the others. Initially upset, he was reeled in by Sketches’ promise of more information.

Skills

Like his twin, LP is good with computers on his own (though tends to lean on his Stand if he can’t figure something out). He also has a secret love of gardening, dutifully tending to a tiny herb garden in his and Liam’s home. Him and his twin have a sort of “twin telepathy” and can sense when the other is near or feeling strong emotions.

Stand - Electric Friend

Electric Friend is a sentient and high-energy Stand, mirroring its user’s ravenous desire for information. However, its sentience seems to have boundaries, typically only caring about its orders, the information it retrieves and the occasional quip. It’s ability is that it can retrieve any information quickly from the Internet (though more obscure queries might take more time). This makes it a fantastic reconnaissance Stand. To facilitate this ability, it can also travel through electric lines and has a wide radius that it can travel from its user. It’s physical prowess is limited and while it can defend LP in a pinch, he doesn’t often fight with it.

Electric Friend interacts in a curious way with Paranoid Android, the former able to plug its plug-like tail into a port on Paranoid Android’s chest. When this happens, Paranoid’s speed and strength are augmented and Electric Friend can help guide Paranoid. Visually, the pair switch color highlights, with Paranoid’s red highlights turning green and Electric Friend’s green highlights turning red. Before initiating, the twins must be in complete agreement and “in-tune” with the other. This isn’t an ability the twins can use often however, as they become exhausted after the pair disconnect, leaving them vulnerable.

Relationships

Liam Piccoli

After the incident at the lake, LP is fiercely protective of his brother. He feels terribly guilty about what happened that day, though he doesn't admit it. He just knows that Liam could have died and it would have been on his hands. However, he does sometimes lack the awareness to see how his twin really feels about some things.
