Nova has it hard to stay still or even stay in one place. She just wants to go, go, go. She does return home but even then, rarely stays long.

Personality

Nova is a wild soul, never content to stay still or even in one place. She has a wanderlust that calls her away to new places and to always keep moving. She’s an adrenaline junkie who likes to do new things, even risky things, just for the experience. She lives in the moment and thinks one should value the present just as much as the past and future. She’s very worldly and when she calms down, you’ll find she is wise beyond her years.

History

Nova was born into a well-off family who was pretty settled down. Her teenage years found her restless and ready and raring to go. With a reluctant blessing, her parents saw her off and she’s been roaming since. She does make sure to come home, visiting both her parents and her sibling Vice. When she does stay in a place for more than a couple of months, its often with her parents or sibling. When she’s with Vice, she helps around the Hibiscus Lounge as a favor to them.

Skills

Despite her size, she’s good at getting herself out of fights. While she never starts them, she’s really good at finishing them.

She has a good singing voice though she doesn’t sing often.

Trivia

Her main mode of transportation is a motorcycle that she was gifted as an 18th birthday gift by her sibling.
