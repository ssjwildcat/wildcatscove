Curse is quite possibly the brightest person you’ll meet and definitely gonna be the kindest one you’ll meet. Despite the struggles he does go through, he’s more than content in where he’s gotten with his family and all.

Personality

Curse is an optimistic and generally upbeat person. He likes to see others happy and strives to help those around him be happy, often being very generous. He’s extremely diligent in all he does, striving for perfection, something that stems from his OCD. His self-esteem tends to be based on this and while he doesn’t necessarily have low self-esteem, how he views himself is rooted in how he views his works. He finds anything more social than a day with his friends nerve-wracking and while he can cope usually, he wishes he could be somewhere else. Despite this, he comes off as a very easy person to talk to and he often is. He tends to be naive and despite being smart, can be slow to realize things.

He tends to overworry but dislikes it when others know how much he does. When he was younger, he’d flat out want to fake his happiness but between disappointment from his mother and wife, he tries to be more genuine with his less positive emotions.

History

Curse is a single child to his mother Holly and a man she frankly regrets having a child with. His father wasn’t a good person and left the whole family a wreck, Curse being self-conscious and anxious in particular. When he was still young, his mother broke it off with him and moved away with Curse.

Curse moved out when he was twenty, having a somewhat stable job in his Let’s Play channel LuckyCatCurse. He was able to move into a nice place and continued work on his own channel and one he collabs with his friends Frostbite and Kai, who he met when he was eighteen.

Late that next year, when he was twenty-one, his friends dragged him to a holiday party, under the guise of helping him socialize. When they ended up doing their own things, Curse ended up drifting to Saphire, who was there under similar circumstances. The two bonded fast, especially since Saphire had found an escape from her eccentric (but somewhat well-meaning) father in him.

What started out as a fast friendship turned into a romantic relationship. It wasn’t for even another year when Curse proposed and they were engaged. Nowadays, the two are happily married with kids Kyle, Esmeralda and Emmanuel.

Skills

He, in general, is very intelligent and is able to grasp things very quickly. He’s got a natural knack for technology, able to build things on his own.

While Curse is a cyber demon, his earthly origins make him closer to human. Thus, he lack a lot of the powers he’d be afforded if he spent time in Cyberspace. He however has a minor ability to influence electricity and electronics that he really only uses to fix things.

He’s not a half bad cook and baker and often does it more out of him and his wife.

He can play a piano, albeit not professionally.

Trivia

He has OCD and is on the autistic spectrum.

In regards to his OCD; it manifests in a desire of keeping things neat and orderly so his life reflects that. Things he percieves as disorder distresses him. He tends to double-lock things just be sure. This also manifests in his perfectionism and he often subconsciously links his self-worth to his works.

In regards to his autism; loud noises don’t often bother him as he’s loud himself (often without realizing!). He likes soft things best but things that bother him include velvet, sandpaper, silky fabrics and things with irregular bumps. His tail tends to be the focus of most of his stims; swishing it really quickly, moving/twisting it in various ways, tapping it in time, etc. You can tell if he’s in deep thought by looking at his tail and if it’s moving.

Due to him being a cyber demon, he is mostly immune to Earth-borne illnesses. Minor things that colds can still affect him. He is, however, not immune to illnesses that can target cyber beings.

He is tone-deaf and can’t really sing as a result. He can, however, keep a tune by humming.

He can roll his r’s.

He LOVES puns. Bad, good, clever, dumb, he knows it and he will tell you it.

While his vision is not terrible, he still needs glasses for reading and driving.

He is trilingual; English being his first language, Spanish being his second which he can speak pretty well and French which he is rusty in.

His nickname “Curse” stems from an inside joke between him and Frostbite, from when he insisted Curse was a curse on him as he stole all his good luck. This further inspired his channel’s name “LuckyCatCurse”.

He doesn’t know how much stock he puts in luck but does tend to have very good luck.

A cherished item of his is a floppy and orange plush cat that was one of the few Christmas gifts he got the year after his parents separated. It’s his favorite stuffed animal from his childhood and a comfort item as a result.

Curse was created in 2013 as an “Evil Youtuber” version of Chuggaaconroy, named Chuggaacurseroy and made such by LP. After a while, I decided to divorce the character from the initial concept.

Design History

V.1, June 2013: The original design! Curse, starting off as an “Evil Youtuber” of Chuggaaconroy, was concepted as a sort of “cyber demonic” version of him that had been corrupted by an anthromorphized LP Curse. So there’s loads of references to his LPs (probably to his design’s detriment…) with a Pikachu tail for Pokemon, Link’s hat for Legend of Zelda, Mario’s lil suspender thing for well...Mario, and the red markings for Okami. The red striped shirt was pretty common in fanart and some funky arm “corruption”, claws and demon eyes round it out.

V.2, October 2013: I got really attached to Curse fast, hence why his design went through a lot of changes fast. Here I swapped out the Pikachu tail for a jereboa-like tail. I don’t really remember my reasoning except I like that trait. The orange fur was probably to further complicate his design since he was supposed to look a lil chaotic (once again, to his design’s detriment…). His eyes changed here to add a green ring around the pupils and he got a few more Amaterasu markings.

V.3, December 2013: Last change for a bit! I ditched his shoes and gold buttons on the suspenders because the former was awkward looking and the later I just never remembered to draw.

V.4, April 2016: The great unfuckening of his design and around when I decided to start moving away from his origin. He gets a healthy dose of melanin as he was quite literally ghostly white which didn’t track with someone part Mexican. He loses the blue torn suspender-thing, the marking on his hat’s...badge? The markings got shuffled around; some I scrapped, others I added. The markings on his arms are now fur. A friend of mine interpreted the markings as that a couple years prior and I liked it so much more; it just took until I redraw his reference next for them to appear on one. His hair gets darkened to black because me and my buddy realized Chuggaaconroy has black hair, not brown and we just hadn’t quite bit the bullet entirely on severing the two’s connection. Finally, peep the ring! He loves his wife.

V.5, April 2019: Not much change here but you can see me scooting out of my comfort zone and making him chubbier.

V.6, June 2020: More continued scooting out of my comfort zone and making him chubbier…

V.7, May 2023: MAKE! THAT! MAN! FATTER! And uh...transer! You can’t tell but he’s got a sick set of lightning-bolt top scars under that shirt! Like all good OC developments, me and my buddy Jamison were talking about how much cooler and hotter he’d be as a trans man. Then I realized...he’s my OC and I can do whatever the fuck I want! Other more minor changes include a mole under his right eye, pointed, red-tipped ears, and a pair of gay lil earrings, despite him being a straight man.

V.8, June 2024: Make that man even fatter!!! I remember drawing this in a frenzy before Artfight because someone in the server said he wasn’t quite fat enough for what they were looking for. I was a little hurt since I had really tried but decided to give it another try and I’m quite pleased!
