Tabirael is a guardian angel, albiet a frustrated one. They never seem to make any lasting impact and they hate it. So they’ve cast themselves from Heaven to make a more direct impact, though at the cost of their connection to Heaven.

Personality

Tabirael is a cheerful angel, even moreso than their usually stoic brethren. They have a big heart and often treat everyone kindly, even those who do not deserve it. Despite being infinitely wise, they come off as naive on Earth, as they do not know a lot of human affairs. Their perception of humans as a result is terribly shallow but they are willing to learn and be helpful. They come off as blunt as well but its because they see little reason as masking their words.

History

Tabi’s role in the angelic hierarchy is that of an guardian angel, destined to watch over humans and guide them, albiet from a distance. They felt frustrated often with how bad things still happened with their charges or when they didn’t listen to them. Finally, their frustration came to a peak and, in an impulsive choice, they willingly cast themselves to Earth, wishing to be closer to their charge and help in a more direct way. Luckily, their sibling Gavirel followed to help them with the new place.

Skills

As an angel, albeit a fallen one, he has many abilities. He has shapeshifting abilities, using them mostly to shift between human and angel and an in-between form.

They can warp others’ perception and change what they perceive, which they use to mitigate their limited shapeshifting ability. They have telepathic and telekinetic powers. They have the ability to teleport.

As an angel, he’s very durable, able to withstand many injuries. Even incapacitated, its impossible for him to die unless under specific conditions. He can regenerate back from all blows, though it takes more time as he’s fallen.

Trivia

Tabi loves music and favors soft music. They lean towards classical but also like ambient and jazz, among other genres.

Tabi’s favorite bird is the pigeon! They like their varied patterns.

Tabirael is an expy of Kaworu Nagisa from Neon Genesis Evangelion. His angelic design is based off a design I did for a more Angelic Kaworu. His name itself is meant to be similar to "Tabris".
