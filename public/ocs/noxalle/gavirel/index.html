Gav, on the surface, is someone who doesn’t take anything seriously and likes to indulge. But when his brother fell willingly, he followed, hinting that he has deep loyalties and that his playful persona may be hiding more…

Personality

Gavirel is a laid-back, quick-talking and often sarcastic angel. He’s mischievous and likes to mess with people. He’s playful and hedonistic, very much ‘living in the moment’. He isn’t above petty actions and even relishes in them if he really doesn’t like the person. If he feels they need it, he enjoys taking others down a peg. At the end of the day, he hides an eerily serious side that makes him a terrifying person when needed. Unlike the vast majority of angels, who are wholly ambivalent in humanity, he is fascinated by them and has a vested interest in their safety.

History

What exactly Gav’s role in the heaven hierarchy is somewhat nebulous. He claims to be a messenger, able to flit between Earth and heaven at will. He doesn’t always go to Earth for his duties, but often flits down to play mischief on humans. While he is mostly harmless, those he feels need “retribution” often find their sins tossed back at them; a just desserts if you will.

This ability to come and go between Earth came in handy when his sibling Tabirael fell willingly, having felt they were going to do more good on Earth. Thus, Gavirel was able to follow, without falling himself.

Skills

As an angel, he has many abilities. He has shapeshifting abilities, using them mostly to shift between human and angel and an in-between form. However, he can shift to look like other people.

He has conjuration abilities, able to conjure all sorts of objects. He can warp others’ perception and change what they perceive. He has telepathic and telekinetic powers. He has the ability to teleport.

As an angel, he’s very durable, able to withstand many injuries. Even incapacitated, its impossible for him to die unless under specific conditions. He can regenerate back from all blows.

Trivia
