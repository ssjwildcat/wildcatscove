Personality

Valentine is positively perky and happy—so much so she even comes off as ditzy and naive. She’s not stupid, she just doesn’t think things through. She is hyperactive and super sociable, liking to talk with people and spent time.

History

Valentine owns a small hotel with her girl Willow in a small desert town. The hotel is public-facing but to fellow Noxchildren, it’s a safe haven and refuge where they will be safe and not face judgement.

Skills

Being a sociable person, Valentine takes care of the people-facing aspects of manning the hotel. She also has a knack for baking and enjoys it.
