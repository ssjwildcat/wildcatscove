Personality

Van’s original programming made him a somewhat mischievous, playful and edgy character. But now, broken down and malfunctioning, he’s become irritable and violent. His emotions are unstable, swinging from cheerful to hostile in moments. He’s distrustful of just about everyone but children, who he’s surprisingly gentle and protective over. He’s a loner who likes to be by himself but his companions would find him aggressively loyal to them.

History

Van was concepted as a rival racer to Roxy for her attraction; a more “edgy” racer who wasn’t above dirty tricks to win. He was adapted after he was created as a security animatronic, with plans to reprogram him to his original purpose to debut later. It was one night he was patrolling that he was damaged. A burglar had broke in to steal important documents on a USB and, when Van tried to stop him, smashed him with a bat. Damaged, he was planned to be fixed.

The burglar returned during the day and was found by Van, who had been roaming in the back hallways. Suddenly furious, Van viciously attacked him, mauling him to death. He was decommissioned and stashed in a backroom, forgotten. He still secretly roams around during the night, missing the attractions and other animatronics who now distrust and fear him.

Skills

Van can more than defend himself and others, using his claws, teeth and body weight. He even keeps his claws sharp in case he needs to fight someone. Due to being decommissioned early on, he is built off older technology and disconnected from the network, meaning he is immune to any threats on the network. He enjoys to sing though isn’t very good due to his un-tuned voice box.
