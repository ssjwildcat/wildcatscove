Kaiick Briefs

Age 19
Species Saiyan/human hybrid
Gender Female
Pronouns she/her
Orientation Bisexual

A child of Vegeta and Bulma that never was, Kaiick is a member of the Multiversal Travelers, a sister force to the Time Patrol. Like her father, she wants to rise above all else but unlike him, she is willing, albeit begrudgingly, to accept those below her.

Personality

Kaiick fits the Saiyan stereotype of being loud, brash and rude. She’s stubborn and doesn’t know when to quit, often holding her ground past what is needed. Despite her boasts of strength, she’s more brains than brawn, cleverly using her wits to keep her ahead. She’s a very stern and demanding boss, making those under her dub her “The Drill Sergeant”. She has an ego that demands all see her as cool and powerful, often taking great insult to otherwise. In truth, she is hyper-aware she was once weak than those typically of her station and has had to fight and claw her way to where she is now. She is annoyed by slackers and lazy people.

History

Kaiick is a part of the Multiversal Travelers, a group that spans the multiverse and is occupied with protecting it and all worlds within it. She is a character that “never was”, being a third child of Bulma and Vegeta from a destroyed alternate universe. When she was brought into the fold, she fought her way to the top, becoming a senior member of the Dragon World Division.

There at the top, she met another high-ranking member, Kenkoh, an upbeat guy with an effortless power. However, he never seemed to take anything seriously, something that annoyed Kaiick to no end and took as a personal slight.. She swore to rise above him, something he took as a challenge. However, as time went on, she found that he wasn’t the layabout she thought him as and is quite the diligent agent. While she still wants to rise above him, she finds him more endearing than annoying now.

Skills

As a Saiyan hybrid, she has an innate sense of ki control. With it, she can fly, use ki in attacks, and sense ki. Her Saiyan genes give her zenkai, which gives her a boost in her power after recovering from battle. She also has super strength, agility, speed, durability and endurance.

As she still has her tail, she could tranform into an Oozaru under a full moon. She can also transform into a Super Saiyan, Super Saiyan 2 and a form called Shadow Saiyan.

Kaiick has her own unique Super Saiyan variant: the Shadow Saiyan. It seems to tap into dark elements and emotions, allowing the user some usage over darkness at the detriment of their mental stability. Because of this, it can be difficult to control and access and shouldn’t be used for long periods of time.

Trivia

Kaiick’s origin is of my first fan-character, just with some reworks. She was from a (now lost) fanfiction I wrote in 2007 called "Prince's Heritage".

Kaiick’s name is pronounced “Kai-yick”.
