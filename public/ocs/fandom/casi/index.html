Casi

Age 29
Species Anthro Cat
Gender Female
Pronouns she/her
Orientation Acearo

Personality

Casi tends to be a loner, preferring the company of a few, close friends. She has a restless energy about her which she satisfies by traveling and seeing the world. While she is slow to anger, she can be quite explosive when she does explode. She always feels guilty after though her pride might make it hard to admit as much. Her friends find her loud and eccentric but ultimately genuine. She has a confidence that she can learn anything she puts her mind to which often leads to her only learning what she needs to before moving on.

History

Casi has always been gripped with restlessness, ever since she was born in her small mountain village. As an adult, she satisfied it by striking out as a nomad, taking up odd jobs and settling in places for a time before she moves on. While she always needs money, she can also be satisfied with local legends, as she is fascinated by myths and legends.

For the time being, she’s settled in and around West City, where she has a delivery job.

Skills

Casi has picked up a hang of ki in her travels, having spent time in the same mountain town as Tien and his dojo. Even though she moved on, she had a grasp enough on the basics to develop her own fighting style which relies on flashy albeit risky moves; moves that hurt when they hit but equally likely to blow up in her face.

Trivia

Casi’s themed after luck, gambling and casinos. Her colors are based off playing cards and the green in her design calls to mind blackjack tables.
