Gender Male
Pronouns he/him
Profile Credit polarized-disguise

Personality

Jasper’s programmed personality was to be a mystical and mysterious fortuneteller, always vague about everything. However, he grew to be anxious and shy. His fortunes suddenly became very ominous, which in turn made him as such.

He doesn’t tend to be hostile but after he was scrapped, he began to attack intruders who came into his home.

History

Jasper was created for the now defunct Rattlesnake Saloon established in the mid nineties, intended to fill the space Freddy Fazbear’s had left behind after their decline after ‘93. He was intended to be a fortuneteller, telling a pre-generated list of fortunes, none of which were supposed to be true. However, for one reason or another, he became too good of a fortuneteller, tapping into the supernatural. He started to see spirits and tell extremely accurate fortunes, which startled and scared many. This left him dejected and sad, even worse after he was scrapped, being blamed for their lack of success and left in Parts and Services. His friend and other animatronic Rita often visited him and he told her what he’d see.

One night, he told her of someone who would come in the next day that was dangerous. True to his word, a shady man came in the next day that got to close to the children. When he got too close to the stage with a child, Rita acted fast and bit him. While he didn’t suffer any greivous wounds and was proven to be a predator, the restaurant closed. Nowadays, he lies abandoned in an old warehouse with others who met the same fate including Rita.

Skills

He was programmed to tell fortunes, reading from a pre-programmed list of fortunes. He also had the ability to converse with customers and walk around, though he had little reason to leave his booth. For some reason, Jasper can see ghosts and tell extremely accurate fortunes, giving him the ability to predict the future.
