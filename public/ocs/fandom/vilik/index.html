Personality

Vilik has skewed morals and overall is a little unhinged. He takes joy in inciting violence and annoying people. He tends to be nosy but considering he doesn’t mind it when things blow up in his face, it’s not likely he will stop soon. He’s stubborn and determined to a fault, often crossing boundaries to get what he wants. What he often wants is knowledge as he likes to learn new things, especially things deemed forbidden and forgotten. Oddly enough, he rarely angers. When he’s not around others and bothering them, he’s quiet and brooding.

History

An alien from a planet long-forgotten and long-destroyed, Vilik has forgotten the details of his former life except for the bare minimum. His planet had been pilfered by an early iteration of the Cold Empire, his people massacred in the genocide their soldiers wrought. Terrified of death, Vilik called to the demonic god he followed for salvation. In a cruel twist, he was changed and spirited away, tasked to gather the god more followers.

For a time, Vilik obeyed but was tormented by every person he failed to convert with the god’s taunting voice and cacophonous noise inside his head. Eventually, he refused to do the god’s bidding anymore, leaving himself the god’s sole believer. In time and with determination, the god’s voice faded from his mind and Vilik found himself free to do as he liked.

He spent years roaming the universe, taking each day at a time. He never wasted a chance to take a pot shot at the Cold Empire and is delighted to find they’ve declined in recent years.

In recent years, he’s met and been intrigued by Gailan. Not only is it because he’s a Saiyan but also his demeanor. Rumor around the universe says the Saiyans have been reduced to a handful at best and its a fact that their former planet is nothing but an asteroid field which makes his existence curious. Combine that with the fact Gailan is cautious and mellow, Vilik is determined to get answers out of him, one’s the Saiyan doesn’t have. This has led to quite a few battles between the two. So much so, Vilik has trailed Gailan and his troupe to Earth...

Skills

Vilik has an innate sense of ki control, using it to fly, use ki blasts and beams. He’s a glass cannon with high strength, speed and agility but can’t take a hit. Due to his curious condition however, he has heightened endurance. This condition also allows him to be functionally immortal; he doesn’t age and will heal from any wound.

Trivia

He speaks with a slight hiss to his voice.
