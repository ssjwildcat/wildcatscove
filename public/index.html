About Me

What is there to say about me? I go by Wildcat and I’m a still-questioning-but-we’ll-say-woman 29-year-old from the good ol’ US of A (/s). I’m a hobbyist artist, writer and now-coder who’s always wanted my own website and finally said, screw it, let’s jump right in. As a result, the coding might appear wonky or weird in places since I’m teaching myself as I go. For something a bit more in-depth, check the about me and about the site pages!
